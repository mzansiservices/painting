About Our Company

Quality painting services with a personal touch

Simon Simon

Founder & Lead Painter

With over 15 years of experience in the painting industry, Simon Simon established Simunye Construction in 2015 with a vision to provide high-quality, reliable painting services to the Mamelodi community and surrounding areas.

Simon began his career as an apprentice painter and worked his way up through various painting companies before starting his own business. His hands-on approach and attention to detail ensure that every project meets his exacting standards.

"My philosophy is simple - treat every home as if it were my own. Quality workmanship and customer satisfaction are the foundations of our business."
